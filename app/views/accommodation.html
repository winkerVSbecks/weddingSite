Getting Here & Getting Around Toronto

The best way to get around in downtown Toronto is TTC (public transit), taxi, or to stretch your legs and walk!

The subway system in Toronto is not extensive, but streetcars run along the main east-west streets in the downtown (King St, Queen St, and Dundas St). The TTC also has a very useful route-planning tool on its website TTC Trip Planner.

Both Beck Taxi and Hailo offer smart phone apps to hail cabs. The Hailo app also allows you to pay with your phone. They both work well and are easy to use.
